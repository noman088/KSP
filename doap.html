DOAP Curriculum

Module 1: Introduction to Office Automation (1 Month)

Computer Fundamentals

  • History and generations of computers.
  • Components: Hardware, Software, and their classification.
  • Input/output devices and storage systems.

Operating Systems

  • Basics of operating systems (Windows/Linux).
  • File and folder management.
  • Desktop settings, control panels, and troubleshooting.

Digital Literacy

  • Basics of internet and email usage.
  • Creating and managing email accounts.
  • Safe online practices and cybersecurity basics.

Module 2: Office Tools (2 Months)

Word Processing (MS Word)

  • Creating, editing, and formatting documents.
  • Using tables, images, and charts.
  • Mail merge and document security.

Spreadsheets (MS Excel)

  • Introduction to spreadsheets and their uses.
  • Applying formulas and functions.
  • Data sorting, filtering, and visualization using charts.

Presentations (MS PowerPoint)

  • Designing and editing presentations.
  • Adding animations and transitions.
  • Customising slide designs and templates.

Database Management (MS Access)

  • Basics of databases and table creation.
  • Managing records and generating reports.
  • Querying data using SQL basics.

Module 3: Desktop Publishing (2 Months)

Graphic Design Basics

  • Introduction to design principles and colour theory.
  • Working with tools like CorelDRAW or Adobe Illustrator.
  • Designing business cards, brochures, and flyers.

Image Editing

  • Using Adobe Photoshop or similar software.
  • Basic image editing: cropping, resizing, and enhancing.
  • Designing banners, posters, and social media graphics.

Page Layout and Design

  • Introduction to Adobe InDesign or similar tools.
  • Creating page layouts for magazines and newspapers.
  • Working with text styles, columns, and images.

Module 4: Accounting Software (1 Month)

Tally ERP Basics

  • Overview of Tally and its interface.
  • Recording transactions using vouchers.
  • Managing ledgers, balance sheets, and P&L statements.

GST and Taxation

  • GST configuration in Tally.
  • Filing GST returns and tax reporting.

Payroll Management

  • Employee records and salary processing.
  • Generating payslips and statutory compliance.

Module 5: Practical Training and Projects (1 Month)

Real-World Applications

  • Designing a small company logo and letterhead.
  • Creating an invoice and budget sheet in MS Excel.
  • Designing a small magazine or pamphlet.

Final Project

  • Combining office automation and publishing tools for a complete project.
  • E.g., Designing a company profile, including logo, invoices, and brochures.

Learning Outcomes

  • Proficiency in Office Tools: MS Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Access.
  • Graphic Design and Publishing: Skills in CorelDRAW, Photoshop, and InDesign.
  • Practical Accounting: Managing business finances using Tally ERP.
  • Career-Ready Skills: Hands-on experience with tools required for office automation and publishing roles.
