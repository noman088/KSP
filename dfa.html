Accounting Curriculum

Module 1: Fundamentals of Accounting

  • Basics of Accounting
  • Accounting Principles and Concepts
  • Accounting Cycle
  • Types of Accounts and Transactions
  • Journal Entries
  • Ledger Posting
  • Trial Balance
  • Final Accounts
  • Profit and Loss Account
  • Balance Sheet Preparation

Module 2: Advanced Financial Accounting

  • Depreciation Accounting
  • Bank Reconciliation Statement
  • Partnership Accounting
  • Admission, Retirement, and Dissolution
  • Company Accounts
  • Issue and Redemption of Shares/Debentures
  • Inventory Management and Accounting

Module 3: Taxation

Basics of Taxation

  • Direct Taxes (Income Tax)
  • Indirect Taxes (GST)
  • Income Tax Computation
  • Salary, House Property, Business Income, and Capital Gains

GST

  • Registration, Filing, and Returns
  • Input Tax Credit

Module 4: Payroll and Statutory Compliance

  • Payroll Accounting
  • Provident Fund (PF) and Employee State Insurance (ESI)
  • Professional Tax
  • TDS (Tax Deducted at Source)

Module 5: Computerised Accounting

Tally ERP/Prime

  • Company Creation and Management
  • Voucher Entries (Sales, Purchase, Payments, Receipts)
  • GST and TDS in Tally
  • Budgeting and Cost Centres

MS Excel

  • Advanced Formulas and Functions
  • Data Analysis and Pivot Tables

Accounting Software (Optional)

  • QuickBooks
  • Zoho Books

Module 6: Auditing

  • Introduction to Auditing
  • Internal and External Audits
  • Auditing Standards
  • Verification of Assets and Liabilities

Module 7: Financial Management

  • Basics of Financial Management
  • Cash Flow and Fund Flow Statements
  • Budgeting and Forecasting
  • Ratio Analysis

Practical Training

  • Hands-on practice with accounting software
  • GST and Income Tax Return filing
  • Preparation of Financial Statements
  • Case Studies and Real-Life Scenarios

Final Project

  • Creation of a complete set of financial statements for a business.
  • GST and Income Tax Return Filing.
  • Use of Tally or other accounting software for project demonstration.

Optional Modules (Institute Specific)

  • E-commerce Accounting
  • Basics of Investment Accounting
  • Banking and Finance Basics
