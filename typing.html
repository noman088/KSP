Week 1: Basics of Typing

  • Introduction to Typing
  • Importance of typing skills
  • Ergonomics and correct posture
  • Finger positioning on the keyboard (home row keys)
  • Typing Practice
  • Typing small words and basic sentences
  • Familiarity with the QWERTY layout

Week 2: Speed and Accuracy Building

  • Typing Techniques
  • Improving accuracy through repetitive practice
  • Typing without looking at the keyboard
  • Practice Sessions
  • Typing common phrases
  • Short paragraphs and number entry
  • Progress Tracking
  • Speed (measured in words per minute, WPM)
  • Error rate reduction

Week 3: Advanced Typing Skills

  • Typing Complex Content
  • Sentences with punctuation marks
  • Handling numbers and special characters
  • Typing Practice Software
  • Familiarity with typing tutors (e.g., TypingMaster, KeyBlaze)
  • Speed drills and accuracy tests

Week 4: Formatting and Real-Life Practice

  • Typing Documents
  • Formatting text in Word processors (e.g., MS Word, Google Docs)
  • Paragraph alignment, bullet points, and numbering
  • Typing for Data Entry
  • Numeric and alphanumeric data entry
  • Filling forms and tables

Week 5: Typing for Office Tasks

  • Typing Business Documents
  • Letters, memos, and emails
  • Reports and meeting agendas
  • Typing Speed Challenges
  • Competing with pre-set WPM goals
  • Increasing typing fluency with large texts

Week 6: Mastery and Final Assessment

  • Typing Proficiency Tests
  • Timed tests for speed and accuracy
  • Error correction exercises
  • Final Project
  • Typing and formatting a comprehensive document
  • Certificate Preparation
  • Recording WPM and accuracy level for course completion

Tools and Resources

Typing Tutors:

  • Free Online Platforms: Typing.com, 10fastfingers.com
  • Software: TypingMaster, Rapid Typing

Practice Material:

  • Pre-designed paragraphs and sentences
  • Typing games for fun learning
