Tally ERP Curriculum

Module 1: Introduction to Tally ERP

  • Overview of Tally ERP software.
  • Installation and setup of Tally ERP.
  • Understanding the Tally interface.
  • Company creation, alteration, and deletion.
  • Basics of accounting principles and double-entry systems.

Module 2: Accounting Basics in Tally

Chart of Accounts:

  • Creating and managing ledgers.
  • Grouping of accounts for categorization.

Voucher Entry:

  • Types of vouchers: payment, receipt, journal, contra, and sales.
  • Recording and editing transactions.

Day Book and Trial Balance:

  • Viewing and managing day-to-day transactions.
  • Generating and analyzing trial balance reports.

Final Accounts:

  • Preparation of profit and loss statements.
  • Creating balance sheets.

Module 3: Inventory Management

  • Creating stock groups, categories, and items.
  • Unit of measure and godown management.
  • Stock vouchers: stock journal, delivery notes, and receipt notes.
  • Inventory tracking and reorder levels.

Module 4: Taxation in Tally

Goods and Services Tax (GST):

  • GST configuration in Tally.
  • GST-compliant invoices.
  • Filing GST returns through Tally.

TDS (Tax Deducted at Source):

  • Enabling and managing TDS.
  • Deduction and payment entries.

Payroll Taxation:

  • Setting up employee tax deductions.

Module 5: Payroll Management

  • Employee master creation and salary details.
  • Managing attendance, leaves, and overtime.
  • Salary processing and payslip generation.
  • Provident Fund (PF) and Employee State Insurance (ESI) configuration.

Module 6: Advanced Features in Tally

Cost Centres and Profit Centres:

  • Allocating expenses and income.
  • Generating cost centre reports.

Budgeting and Controls:

  • Setting up budgets.
  • Monitoring variances.

Bank Reconciliation:

  • Importing and reconciling bank statements.
  • Cheque printing and tracking.

Multi-Currency Transactions:

  • Configuring currencies.
  • Recording foreign exchange transactions.

Module 7: Reports and Analysis

Generating accounting reports:

  • Balance Sheet, P&L Statements, Cash Flow, and Fund Flow reports.

Statutory reports:

  • GST, TDS, and tax summary reports.

Management reports:

  • Stock summary, ageing analysis, and cost centre reports.

Module 8: Data Security and Backup

  • Configuring user roles and permissions.
  • Setting up data security with passwords.
  • Data backup and restore process.
  • Synchronization and remote access in Tally.

Module 9: Practical Training

  • Real-world scenarios and case studies.
  • End-to-end accounting for a sample company.
  • GST filing and payroll processing simulation.

Optional Topics (Advanced Modules)

  • Digital Payments Integration: Linking Tally with payment gateways.
  • Customisation in Tally: Basics of TDL (Tally Definition Language).
